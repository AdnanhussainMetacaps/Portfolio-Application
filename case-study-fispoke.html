The Challenge

Fragmented Financial Services

Financial advisors faced significant hurdles in managing diverse client needs across multiple banking products. Traditional processes were manual, required redundant data entry, and lacked a unified tracking system for high-yield savings (HYSA), mortgages, and complex securities-based loans.

The need was for a "single pane of glass" platform that combined enterprise-grade security with an intuitive mobile-first experience, allowing advisors to launch applications and track client liquidity in real-time.
